# Xenia — Product Intelligence Standard

*Constitutional, not implementation. Governs how the product anticipates,
accelerates, and assists — the layer benchmark products call "power
features". Bounded by the Manifesto (Doc 12), the never-automatic floor
(Doc 02), the receipt rule (Doc 05), and backend-only business logic
(AP5). Principles numbered I1–I11, citable in review. Implemented by the
Intelligence phase of the Evolution Plan and by nothing before it.*

---

## I1 — The command palette is the colleague's ear, not a menu dump

One invocation (⌘K), one field, and it accepts **the loop's own verbs and
nouns** — "pursue Brightpath", "correct: no franchises", "open this
week", "export the DNA" — not a flattened sitemap. Ranking is by the
loop's rhythm (this week's queue outranks settings, always), never by
usage-mined popularity. The palette may *stage* any action but
**consummates nothing irreversible**: destructive or outward-facing acts
route to their own surface with their own friction. If a command exists
in the palette but not as a visible affordance somewhere, that is a bug —
the palette accelerates the product; it is never the only door.

## I2 — Keyboard-first means reachable-first

Every interaction reachable without a pointer; the loop's daily verbs get
single keys (j/k traversal, enter to open, 1–6 for chips, e to export,
esc always retreats); shortcuts are discoverable in place (hover/help,
not a manual) and never override text entry. The test: a founder does
Monday's ten minutes without touching the mouse and without having
learned anything except by being shown at the moment of relevance.

## I3 — Search is recall over receipts, not discovery over noise

Search answers "where is the thing I know exists" — a business, a brief,
a DNA statement, a receipt's claim — scoped to the workspace's own world
and ranked deterministically (exact > name > claim text > recency).
Search never becomes a research surface (directed research is C3's job,
with politeness and budgets); never surfaces another tenant's anything;
and never answers with AI prose — it returns *objects*, each carrying its
receipts. If a query looks like a hunt ("agencies in Leeds hiring"), the
answer is a handoff to research, honestly labelled, never a silent crawl.

## I4 — Intelligent defaults, dumb overrides

Every default is the statistically-kindest choice, and every default is
visible and overridable in place — intelligence chooses the starting
point, never the destination. Defaults may adapt to observed behaviour
only at the pattern threshold (third time — adjusting, Doc 04 §5's rule
generalised to the interface) and always with narration. A default that
cannot explain why it changed reverts to static. The product never moves
a control the user's hand has learned.

## I5 — Progressive disclosure follows the three depths

Ten seconds, two minutes, the audit (Doc 06): every surface leads with
its verdict, unfolds to its reasoning, and grounds out in its receipts —
and **scrolling is the disclosure**; nothing decision-relevant hides
behind hover states, tabs-within-tabs, or interaction gymnastics.
Disclosure is monotone: opening depth never rearranges what shallower
depth showed.

## I6 — Undo is revert-with-log, never erasure

Xenia's memory is constitutional (append-only teaching, total changelog),
so undo is domain-shaped, not ctrl-z-shaped: a DNA change reverts via a
*new* logged event; a decision is reconsidered as a *new* decision where
the domain allows it; teaching events and outcomes are never unsaid —
"I taught it and it forgot" must be unrepresentable. The interface's job
is to make reverting as fast as acting (one affordance, named effect
returned), so append-only *feels* like undo while remaining honest.
Mistyped input before commitment (a draft, an unanswered interview box)
is freely editable — the log begins where meaning begins.

## I7 — Empty states are rooms with furniture

Every empty state answers three questions in Xenia's voice: what would
live here, why it is empty *right now* (integrity, timing, or your move),
and the single next act. First-run emptiness is the concierge's welcome
(homework-first); steady-state emptiness is a report ("nothing met the
bar — here's what I checked"); never a grey void, never an illustration
apologising for the product.

## I8 — Contextual AI assists the sentence you are writing, never the one you aren't

AI appears in context only where judgment is already flowing — refining a
draft, distilling a rambling interview answer *side-by-side with the
verbatim original*, proposing the chip a decline resembles — always
labelled, always declinable, always receipted where material, and always
silent by default (M6). Contextual AI never initiates conversation, never
summarises what the user can read in the time the summary would take, and
never appears as an assistant persona separate from Xenia — there is one
colleague here, not a colleague plus a paperclip.

## I9 — Acceleration compounds; shortcuts don't

Features earn the name "time-saving" only if the minutes return every
week: the queue's one-decision-per-card, the ten-second correction, the
draft that starts from the brief, batch acknowledgment where judgment
genuinely isn't per-item. Anti-scope: automation that removes the human
from acts the constitution reserves (sending, overriding, endorsing) is
not acceleration, it is the never-automatic floor being sanded — refused
regardless of how much time it would save (Doc 02 N3).

## I10 — Anticipation without surveillance creep

Xenia may prepare what the rhythm makes predictable — Monday's queue
warmed, the outcome prompt at the honest interval, the brief refreshed
when staleness crosses its threshold — because these are the employee's
initiative on *work*. She may not mine the founder's interaction telemetry
for psychological timing, urgency windows, or re-engagement hooks. The
line: anticipate the **work**, never the **user**.

## I11 — Delight is competence surfacing, briefly

The permitted delights are the constitution's own moments done perfectly:
the didn't-know-this insight, the visible exclusion, the named effect
landing instantly, the win narrated as learning, doubt declared before
discovery. Delight is never appended (no confetti, no streaks, no
mascots, no "you're crushing it") — it is what excellence feels like when
the product gets out of its way. If a proposed delight would still make
sense in a product that had no judgment in it, it does not belong in this
one.
